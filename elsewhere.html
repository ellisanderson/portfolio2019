Elsewhere Museum

Freelance Design
Non-profit Arts Organization
2014 - Present

Overview

Elsewhere is "a living museum" based in Greensboro, NC, hosting artists from around the world as part of its residency program. Participants use the vast collection of thrift within the massive three-story building to create, recreate and recycle works of art. I've had the honor to work with the team to create design projects for their Brochure and Annual Fundraising Posters in 2014, 2016 and 2017.
